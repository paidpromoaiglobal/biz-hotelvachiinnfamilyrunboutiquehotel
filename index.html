Our Story

Made with Passion

Hotel Vachi Inn is a family-run boutique hotel nestled in the vibrant Malviya Nagar neighbourhood of Jaipur, Rajasthan. We offer simple, neat, and well-maintained rooms designed to make every guest feel at ease, whether you are a leisure traveller exploring the Pink City or a business visitor seeking a comfortable base. Our 24-hour operations ensure you are always looked after, no matter what time you arrive or need assistance.

At Vachi Inn, we take pride in our beautifully crafted interiors, lush greenery, and a rooftop terrace that treats guests to sweeping views of Jaipur's skyline. Our in-house restaurant serves hearty meals, and our dedicated staff is committed to resolving any concern promptly. With over 1,100 guest reviews and a warm, improving track record, we continue to grow and enhance our services to deliver the best value boutique experience in Jaipur.
